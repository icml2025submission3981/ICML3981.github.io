Experimental Results

Exp. A: ImageNet with Flux VAE

Caption: Scaling curves for Diffusion Transformers on ImageNet with Flux VAE. In the first figure, experiments are conducted across a wide range of compute budgets. We fit a parabola for each budgets and find the optimal parameters (lowest point). In the Figure 2-4, we plot the relationship between the optimal model under each compute budget and its pameters, loss, and FID.

Exp. B: Effect of CFG Scale

Caption: We test the compute-optimal models with different CFG Scale (0.0, 2.5, 5.0). The CFG affects the slope of the curve and cause a vertical shift. However, CFG Scale will not affect the existence of scaling laws.

Exp. C: Transferability on more text-2-image dataset

Caption: Scaling curves on JourneyDB and Flickr30k, we test our mode pre-trained from Laion subset on two more text-2-image datasets. The FID and validatin loss on two OOD dataset shows clear power-law relationship with compute budget.

Exp. D: Image Samples with incresing compute

Caption: We sample some images with our compute-optimal models. The image quality improves with increasing compute budgets.

Exp. E: More Text-2-Image metrics

Caption: We test other image-based metric (Gen Eval). We use our compute-optimal models to generate samples given the prompts and test its performance. The score also follows a power-law relationship.
